Hello, this is study easy.

This is study easy, a tool to help you study effectively.
It was made as a micro project for NLP course at Thapar Institute, Patiala by Shivam Behl.
This tools helps you optimise your study sessions by helping you recognize important terms and parts of the content and help you revise with the help of practice questions.
For Question Generation, I have used Part of Speech tagging and made fill ups. While making fill ups, I priority was given to common nouns, however if proper nouns are not available in sentence, the system will use proper nouns.
For making options, synonyms were retrieved using wordnet.
Backend of the website is developed in Flask and frontend using HTML, CSS and Javascript.

This Website is built in a very short period of time, and I am positive about improving it.
